About the team behind the magic

Deezer Research aims at expanding knowledge about music and how people interact with it.

We are a team dedicated to solving complex problems regarding our audio catalog organisation and Human-Music interactions. Our primary mission is to help make Deezer a cutting-edge audio streaming service, tailored to its customers' needs.

Beyond this scope, we regularly share our latest findings in international scientific conferences on domain ranging from Audio Signal Processing to Natural Language Understanding and Recommender systems. We also extensively share code and data to ensure reproducibility of our work and our team is regularly reinforced with PhD, master students and partnerships with academic institutions to tackle complex research problems.
